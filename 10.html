Reservations:
Reservations need to be made at least 24 hours in advance for individual treatments. Walk-ins welcome on first come first serve basis.

Payment Policy:
Visa, MasterCard, Discover, Debit and Gift cards accepted.

Cancellation Policy:
For individual service we require a minimum of 24 hours notice when canceling an appointment or a minimum of 4 hours to rebook. Cancellations for group events must be made 72 hours in advance in order to compensate rescheduling for availability.

Gratuities:
If you are pleased with the service you have received, feel free to give a tip directly to your clinician in cash or credit cards.

Quiet Environment:
As a courtesy to other guests, please maintain a quiet level of conversation. Please turn off or mute Cell phones and pagers, out of respect others.
